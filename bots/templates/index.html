ENTHRONEMENT

Use your C/C++ skills to dethrone your enemy & wear the crown of victory.

- Each player has 10 pieces to start with:
3 knights, 6 Bannermen & a Dragon.
- The chances for movement of your pieces are in the Defense, Offense and Magnetic Lane area.
- Enemy's THRONE area consists of throne,common and defense area.
- Your Pieces in offense area cannot move into your own defense area and vice-versa

KNIGHT


- Move a step in any direction i.e. PLAIN MOVE
- Jump over an Enemy to kill and land a block ahead i.e. JUMP KILL
- Jump over an ally and land a block ahead i.e. CANTER

Bannerman


- Move a step in any direction i.e. PLAIN MOVE
- Jump over an enemy to kill and land a block ahead i.e JUMP KILL

Dragon


- Move a step in any direction i.e. PLAIN MOVE
- Fly over allies in a line and land at the end i.e. FLY HORIZONTAL/VERTICAL
- Kill by Fire i.e. FIRE BREATH

Constraints


- No piece can move or attack backward
- Also every piece move such that it doesn't resist the pull of magnetic lines
- Once inside the side lane, a piece can come out only when it reaches enemy's common area
- A piece in the side lane can only move vertically/diagonally
- However,to move out of a middle lane,use diagonal movement

Constraints


- Dragon cannot attack enemy's defensive pieces unless in their defence area
- If it is in any side lane then it can attack only vertically
- Fire Breath of dragon kills anyone in the two block(Horizontal/Vertical)

How Do i win?


Make two of your offense pieces reach the opponent's iron throne or kill all pieces.
